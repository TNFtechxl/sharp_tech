Video Editing

📘 Definition

Video editing is the process of cutting, arranging, and enhancing video clips to create engaging content for platforms like YouTube, TikTok, and Instagram.

🔥 Importance

Video content dominates the internet today. Businesses, influencers, and brands all depend on high-quality videos to grow their audience and increase sales.

💡 Why You Should Learn It

Learning video editing gives you a powerful skill that is always in demand. You can work from anywhere, build your own content brand, or get paid by clients.

💰 Opportunities

You can earn money by editing for creators, managing social media content, freelancing, or even starting your own video brand online.
