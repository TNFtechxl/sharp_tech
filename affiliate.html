Affiliate Marketing

📘 Definition

Affiliate marketing is a business model where you promote products or services and earn a commission for every sale made through your link.

🔥 Importance

It is one of the easiest ways to start making money online without creating your own product. Many people use it to build passive income.

💡 Why You Should Learn It

You can start with little or no capital. All you need is your phone, internet connection, and the right strategy.

💰 Opportunities

Earn daily commissions, promote global products, and build a long-term online income system.
